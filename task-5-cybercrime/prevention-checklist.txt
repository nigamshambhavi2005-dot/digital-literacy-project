# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone.
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies.
- Do not scan random QR codes sent by unknown people.
- Check URLs carefully before entering sensitive information.

## Financial/UPI Safety Tips:
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money.
- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps.

## General Safety Tips:
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Keep your apps and phone updated regularly.

## Reporting Cybercrime:
- Report incidents at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Helpline: 1930
