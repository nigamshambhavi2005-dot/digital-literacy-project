While researching cybercrime, I was most surprised by how easily scammers manipulate people using simple tricks like fake calls and QR codes. Many victims are not aware that entering a UPI PIN actually sends money instead of receiving it. This shows that lack of awareness is a major reason behind such frauds.

One habit I will personally change is being more cautious while handling online payments. I will never share sensitive information like OTPs or PINs and will always verify unknown requests before taking action. I will also educate my friends and family about these scams so they can stay safe. Developing safe online habits is essential in today’s digital world to avoid financial and personal losses.
