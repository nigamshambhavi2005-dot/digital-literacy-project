# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## ✅ Do's
- Use strong and secure passwords for your accounts.
- Think before posting any content online.
- Respect others and communicate politely.
- Verify information before sharing it.
- Maintain privacy settings on your profiles.

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or arguments.
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation.
